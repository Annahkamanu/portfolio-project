About me

Front-end developer from Nairobi, Kenya

Hello, I'm Ann Kamanu. As an accountant turned web designer, I've embarked on an exciting journey to blend my financial acumen with the world of web design. My web design adventure began earlier this year when I took part in the inspiring workshops offered by SheCodes. Since then, I've been passionately crafting digital experiences that seamlessly merge form and function.

Future adventures

While my current focus is on front-end development and crafting visually stunning static websites, I'm also eager to dive into the world of back-end programming in the near future. The endless possibilities of web development keep me motivated and excited to continue learning and growing in this dynamic field.

Let's Create Together

I believe that the best designs are born from collaboration and a deep understanding of the client's vision. Whether you're an individual looking to establish your online presence or a business seeking to enhance its digital footprint, I'm here to transform your ideas into captivating web experiences. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to the opportunity of working with you to bring your web design dreams to life. Feel free to contact me.
